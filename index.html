When & Where

Wedding Ceremony

Kijfhoek 30, 3335 LE Zwijndrecht.
The route to the church is signposted with blue-and-white signs showing a church and the text: Kijfhoekkerk.
When using the A 16 from Rotterdam or Dordrecht, take exit 23: Hendrik-Ido-Ambacht and Zwijndrecht. Follow the direction Zwijndrecht. At the crossroads with traffic lights, go straight over the railway viaduct to the roundabout. Take the roundabout a quarter turn. Turn right, past the riding school and take the first road on the left. Follow this road, called Kijfhoek. After a sharp turn to the right, the church appears on your left.
